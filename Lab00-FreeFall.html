1. Purpose

The purpose of this laboratory is to examine the acceleration of gravity due to the earth. You will use the kinematic equations to find an experimental value for the gravitational acceleration and then use the kinematic equations to predict the time of flight of a thrown projectile.

\[\text{percent error} = \frac{| \text{experimental value} - \text{accepted value}|}{\text{accepted value}}\]
